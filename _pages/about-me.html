Bio

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at the University of Hong Kong (HKU), and the Principal Investigator of the Network Intelligence and Computing Ecosystem (NICE) Lab @ HKU.

I received my Ph.D. degree from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, and my B.Eng. degree from Beijing Jiaotong University, China. My research focuses on artificial intelligence for next-generation networks, AI-native networked systems, edge intelligence, semantic communications, and reliable agentic AI.
