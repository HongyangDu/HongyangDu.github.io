PI of Network Intelligence and Computing Ecosystem Lab

Hongyang Du

Assistant Professor, HKU EEE | NICE Lab

My group studies AI-native networks and networked intelligence for large-scale, reliable, human-centric digital ecosystems.

I am an assistant professor in the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at the University of Hong Kong.

I lead the Network Intelligence and Computing Ecosystem (NICE) Lab. Our work connects artificial intelligence, communication networks, computing systems, and human-computer interaction, with a current focus on Network for AI and AI for Network.

What We Look For

Passion for learning: strong self-motivation and ability to quickly learn from new fields.

Academic foundation: background in CS, AI, EEE, communications, mathematics, or related fields.

Evidence of readiness: research experience, coding ability, papers, projects, competitions, or a clear research statement.

Working style: responsibility, reliability, and good communication skills.

How to Apply

If you are interested in joining NICE Lab, please email me with a concise research-oriented application.

  • Email: duhy@hku.hk
  • Subject line: PhD/xx Application-University-Name
  • Attachments: CV, undergraduate transcripts, relevant publications, and optional one-page research statement.

Due to email volume, I may not be able to respond to every applicant, but I do read applications and reply to those most relevant to my current research.
