I am an assistant professor at the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, the University of Hong Kong (HKU). I received my Ph.D. degree from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, and my B.Eng. degree from Beijing Jiaotong University, China. I am the Principal Investigator (PI) of the Network Intelligence and Computing Ecosystem (NICE) Lab @ HKU.

From 2025 onward, our lab rethinks the new era of AI and next-generation networks, and identifies the following research projects in NICE:

These projects cover two complementary directions:

  • Network for AI - Unfold Intelligence by Horizontally Scaling It Across the Network
  • AI for Network - Specialize Intelligence by Vertically Optimizing It for Network Systems

They converge toward a unified goal: "Ubiquitous Intelligence, where intelligence is woven into every interaction, every environment, and every moment."

Given the shift in our research style starting in 2025, our methodology and focus have changed. To align our current vision, you may find it in your interest to check our work established from 2025 onwards. Please refer to NICE for openings, research opportunities, and application details.

Selected First-author Papers

  1. "Reinforcement Learning With LLMs Interaction For Distributed Diffusion Model Services", IEEE Transactions on Pattern Analysis and Machine Intelligence, 2025.
    With a distributed generative diffusion model-based AIGC framework, we propose an Interactive AI (IAI) approach, that is, Reinforcement Learning With Large Language Models Interaction (RLLI), which utilizes LLM-empowered generative agents to replicate users interaction, providing real-time and subjective QoE feedback that reflects a spectrum of user personalities.
  2. "AI-Generated Incentive Mechanism and Full-Duplex Semantic Communications for Information Sharing", IEEE Journal on Selected Areas in Communications (Q1, CCF A), 2023. We analyze the performance of full-duplex D2D communications. To facilitate semantic information sharing among users, we design a contract theoretic AI-generated incentive mechanism that generates the optimal contract design, outperforming two DRL algorithms. Code.
    • Selected by IEEE JSAC Popular Documents - October 2023
  3. "Semantic Communications for Wireless Sensing: RIS-aided Encoding and Self-supervised Decoding", IEEE Journal on Selected Areas in Communications (Q1, CCF A), 2023. We propose the inverse semantic communications as a new paradigm. Using the sensing data collected from real-world, we show that our framework can reduce the data volume by 95% compared to that before encoding, without affecting the accomplishment of sensing tasks. Code.
    • Selected by IEEE JSAC Popular Documents - August 2023
  4. "Attention-aware Resource Allocation and QoE Analysis for Metaverse xURLLC Services", IEEE Journal on Selected Areas in Communications (Q1, CCF A), 2023. We propose a novel metric named Meta-Immersion that incorporates both the objective KPIs and subjective feelings of Metaverse users. We develop an attention-aware rendering capacity allocation scheme to improve QoE in xURLLC. Code.
    • Selected by IEEE JSAC Popular Documents - July 2023
    • First Price of Outstanding Papers of Computer Academy of Guangdong (China)
  5. "Diffusion-based Reinforcement Learning for Edge-enabled AI-Generated Content Services", IEEE Transactions on Mobile Computing (Q1, CCF A), 2024. We propose a diffusion model-based AI-generated optimal decision (AGOD) algorithm, which can generate the optimal ASP selection decisions. Code.
